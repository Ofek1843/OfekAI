Refund Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

We want you to be happy with FuelPhysique. If you are not, here is how our refund process works.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to FuelPhysique Pro and are not satisfied for any reason, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase. This applies to first-time Pro subscribers only.

To request a refund, email us at fuelphysiquesupport@gmail.com with the subject "Refund Request" and include your account email address.

2. Renewal Charges

Subscription renewal charges are generally non-refundable. If you intended to cancel before a renewal and were charged, contact us within 48 hours and we will evaluate your request.

3. Other Circumstances

We may issue refunds in exceptional circumstances, including:

  • A billing error or technical error on our part
  • Extended service outages lasting more than 72 hours
  • Duplicate charges

Refunds will not be issued for dissatisfaction with AI-generated content alone.

4. Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed via Stripe and typically appear within 5-10 business days. We will cancel your subscription at the time the refund is issued.

5. Contact

Refund requests: fuelphysiquesupport@gmail.com
