Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026 - Questions? Contact us

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

1. Data We Collect

Account data

Your email address and authentication credentials, managed securely via Firebase Authentication.

Profile data (Athlete Core)

Age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, activity level, dietary preferences, and any health conditions you choose to disclose. All optional, editable at any time.

Workout and nutrition data

Workout plans, logged sessions (exercises, sets, weights, reps, RPE), nutrition plans, and notes.

Progress data

Body measurements, weight logs, and progress photos you upload.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate personalized workout plans, nutrition plans, and AI coaching responses
  • To populate your progress charts and analytics
  • To maintain your workout history and allow photo comparisons
  • To communicate with you about your account and support requests
  • To improve the quality of the Service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), protected by TLS encryption in transit and encryption at rest. Firebase security rules restrict access to your own authenticated account only.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase - Authentication, database, and file storage.
  • AI API providers - Used to power the AI Coach and plan generation. Only fitness-related profile data is sent - no full PII.
  • Stripe - Secure payment processing. FuelPhysique never stores card details.

5. Progress Photos

Progress photos are stored in your private Firebase Storage folder and are not publicly accessible. They are visible only to you when logged in. We do not use your photos for any purpose other than displaying them back to you.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at fuelphysiquesupport@gmail.com.

7. Minors

FuelPhysique does not knowingly collect data from users under 15. Users aged 15-17 may use the Service with parental consent. Contact us immediately if you believe a minor has registered without consent.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: fuelphysiquesupport@gmail.com
