Subscription Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

1. Plans and Pricing

Free tier

  • Workout Builder and Nutrition Builder access
  • Real-time Workout Tracker
  • Basic progress tracking
  • Limited AI Coach messages per month

Pro tier

  • Unlimited workout and nutrition plan generation
  • Unlimited AI Coach messages
  • Progress photo uploads and side-by-side comparisons
  • Advanced analytics and charts
  • Priority support

Current pricing is shown on our Plans page. Prices may change with 30 days notice to existing subscribers.

2. Billing

Pro subscriptions are billed on a recurring monthly or annual basis. Payment is processed securely via Stripe. By subscribing, you authorize recurring charges until you cancel. Your subscription renews automatically at the end of each billing period.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel your Pro subscription at any time from Settings under "Billing". Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period - you retain Pro access until then. No partial refunds are issued for unused time except as outlined in our Refund Policy.

4. Downgrades

When your Pro subscription ends, your account reverts to the Free tier. Your data is retained for at least 90 days and remains accessible when you re-subscribe.

5. Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we will retry the charge. If payment cannot be collected, Pro access is suspended until resolved. You will be notified by email of any failed payments.

6. Contact

Billing questions: fuelphysiquesupport@gmail.com
